Meet the trainer

Owain Williams — man with a whistle

I'm a UK‑based dog trainer who believes that every dog — from the bouncy spaniel pup to the rescue with a difficult past — deserves clear, calm leadership and a chance to thrive.

My approach is honest and unhurried. We start with the relationship, build the foundations, then layer the skills your dog needs to be brilliant company at home, reliable in the field, or steady enough to support someone's wellbeing as a therapy dog.

No gimmicks. No shortcuts. Just consistent, fair training that actually works — for the long run.

— Owain
What I offer

Bespoke training, built around your dog

Every programme is shaped to the dog in front of me — their breed, temperament, history, and the life you want to live together.

General Obedience

The everyday essentials done properly — recall, loose lead walking, place, settle, polite manners and impulse control. A calm, confident dog you can take anywhere.

Gundog Training

Foundation to finished — steadiness, marking, blind retrieves, hunting and quartering. Working dogs and pet gundogs welcome, whether you shoot or just want a switched‑on companion.

Therapy Dog Assessor

Qualified assessment of dogs intended for therapy and visiting work. Temperament, social skills, environmental confidence — rigorously tested, kindly delivered.

The approach

Calm. Consistent. Result‑led.

I won't bully a dog into compliance, and I won't coddle one into confusion. Good training sits in the middle: clear rules, generous rewards, fair consequences, and the patience to repeat things until the dog truly understands.
